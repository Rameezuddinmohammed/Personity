# Product Overview

Personity is an AI-powered conversational research platform that conducts adaptive interviews at scale. The system enables product teams and founders to gather interview-depth insights through dynamic AI conversations.

## Core Value Proposition

- Achieve 70%+ completion rates (vs traditional surveys)
- Provide actionable insights at survey-level cost efficiency
- Enable pause/resume conversations for convenience
- Deliver automated analysis with themes, sentiment, and quotes

## Target Users

- **Creators**: Product teams and founders who create and manage surveys
- **Respondents**: End-users who participate in survey conversations

## Key Features

- 5-step guided survey creation (under 5 minutes)
- AI-powered adaptive conversations using GPT-4o
- Test mode for survey validation
- Per-response and aggregate analysis
- Insights dashboard with export (PDF/CSV)
- Subscription plans: ⏸️ Delayed to Phase 2 (Free tier only for Phase 1)

## Success Metrics

- 70%+ conversation completion rate
- Sub-5 minute survey creation time
- Sub-5 second AI response time (p95)
- Quality score 7+ for completed conversations
